A narrative poem of that year


Today is white

It is not as blue as yesterday

I don't know if tomorrow will be grey

There was a noise from the beacon

A group of crows arrived

They made me envy

Since antiquity

People regard crows as ominous

But they are still free

For them

The only thing they can do is living

Having a nest is enough

Why do they live so comfortable

I asked myself again:

Are you really jealous?

No,I'm not

Humans are adventurous animals

Living comfortably is not what human seek

You must have to work hard

You can be truly free

Here's a cannonball for you

The beginning of writing this short story is the germination of Liu Yu's book---send you a bullet.This book recorded little things of her life for 2005-2009.And I like her book very much.
